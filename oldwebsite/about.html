    • My Pursuit of Knowledge


    My name is Andy Chen and I am currently a Ph.D. student in physics at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My interests lie in theoretical physics, specifically M-theory, and I hope to write my doctoral thesis on topics in this field. I received my B.S. in physics at Georgia Tech in 2018.

    Most of my free time is spent with my friends and family, playing music, cooking, and doing outdoor sports. I have played piano and percussion since elementary school and have fiddled with viola, cello, guitar, and the ukulele. My favorite musical genres are classical and electronic. I also like to experiment in the kitchen. My dishes range from imitations of traditional recipes to unique creations. I enjoy making food for others and encourage feedback, but most of the time I cook because I love to eat. The more popular sports, such as basketball, baseball, football, and soccer, don't usually interest me. However, I love to swim, snowboard, and play ultimate frisbee as well as try out less common outdoor activities.
